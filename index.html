How to use:

1. Put the link in the input box above. Make sure it's valid YouTube URL or it won't work. Also "youtu.be" links won't work so yeah

2. After inputting the link in the box above, click on the download button. It will show you a preview of the picture. Click on the image preview to get the original resolution of it

If you're on mobile, hold down the image and click Save or Download Image.

If you're on computer you can do CTRL + S to save the image or right click and select save as or copy the image to your clipboard.

3. Done! You have downloaded a thumbnail from a YouTube Video!
